About CSE Department

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department at Kingston Engineering College is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education in computer science and engineering. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving field of technology. We offer a range of courses covering programming, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and more. Our faculty members are experienced professionals who are committed to fostering a collaborative learning environment. We also provide opportunities for hands-on experience through projects, internships, and research initiatives.
